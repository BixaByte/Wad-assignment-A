About Royal Computers

Royal Computers is an IT products reselling company focusing on the African Market. Services include sales of Computers, Accesories, Printers, Software, Consumables, Networking Equipment and Technical Support.

At Royal Computers we aim to provide end users with the best option on IT solutions at competitive prices without compromise on quality; hence we sell top quality products from leading vendors and all our products carry at least twelve (12) months warranty, subject to terms and conditions.

For further details, please visit any of our shops nearest to you. Our friendly sales team wll best assist you.

Terms and Conditions

  • Goods returned for credit will be credited at 20% handling fee, but not later than 7 days.
  • All refunds are subject to management’s approval, no refunds on specially ordered computer components.
  • Products are under a 1 year carry – in- warranty as stipulated by the manufacturer, item’ replacement time is determined by the supplier.
